    Prizes & Awards

    2021

    Norris Family Award for Outstanding Author Contribution by a Graduate Research Student to a Published Scholarly Research Output - Monash University, Faculty of Science

    This prize was awarded for my first-author work on the potential formation scenarios for the first intermediate-mass black hole ever confidently detected, signalled by the gravitational-wave signal GW190521: read the paper here.

    2020

    Awarded place in sixth Homeward Bound cohort

    Homeward Bound is a transformational leadership initiative for women in STEMM. As a part of this group, I attend leadership training seminars, workshops and classes. Our training will culminate in a three-week voyage to Antarctica, which was postponed due to the pandemic but is due to go ahead in November 2023! You can read more about Homeward Bound here and read biographies for myself and the other members of the sixth cohort here.
